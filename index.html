About YAUTO
YAUTO (Yet Another Useless Twitch Overlay) is a custom Twitch chat overlay for your broadcasting software. It features integration with popular emote services like 7TV, BetterTwitchTV, and FrankerFaceZ, allowing you to showcase a wide variety of emotes directly on your overlay.
Features
  • Emotes (global/channel/bits), Badges (global/channel)
  • Emotes (global/channel/personal/zero-width), Badges, Paints, WebSocket
  • Emotes (global/channel), WebSocket (Automatic emote updates)
  • Emotes (global/channel), Badges (global/channel)
Commands
  • !reloadoverlay - Mod and higher only, reloads the overlay
  • !refreshoverlay - Mod and higher only, refreshes the overlay
  • !reloadwebsockets - Mod and higher only, reconnects WebSockets
  • !reconnectchat - Mod and higher only, reconnects Twitch IRC
