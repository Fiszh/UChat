YAUTO (Yet Another Useless Twitch Overlay) is a custom Twitch chat overlay for your stream.
It features integration with emote services, including 7TV, BetterTwitchTV and FrankerFaceZ, allowing you to showcase a wide variety of emotes directly on your overlay.
  • Emotes (global/channel/bits), Badges (global/channel)
  • Emotes (global/channel/personal/zero-width), Badges, Paints, WebSocket (Automatic emote updates)
  • Emotes (global/channel), WebSocket (Automatic emote updates)
  • Emotes (global/channel), Badges (global/channel)
